I got bit by the technology bug in the early 90's because of my insistant dad. With him whispering in my ear about new technological advances I was able to establish a solid understanding of computer systems at a young age.
My heart belongs to animals of all species. Most of my exposure has been with dogs and cats. After taking up a career in the pet industry I was able to learn many things about animal handling and pet psychology first hand.
My passion for animals began to grow even more and I dedicated my life to veganism and creating awareness of speciesism. I work closely with the Climate Reality Project in Portland Oregon.
